Yogurt Cake

Description:

Yummy easy yogurt cake to make at home!

For 6 people

Ingredients:

  • 1x bag of baking powder
  • 1x pot of flower
  • 1/2x pot of sugar
  • 1/2x pot of oil
  • 10g of butter
  • 3x eggs
  • 1x yogurt
  • 1x bag of vanilla sugar

Steps:

  1. In this order, add a pot of plain yogurt, the flour, the sugar, and the vanilla sugar. Mix well.
  2. Add the 3 eggs and mix again.
  3. Add the oil, mix, and then add the packet of baking powder.
  4. Mix again until the batter is smooth.
  5. Butter a round cake tin and pour in the batter.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes at 180Â°C (thermostat 6), checking regularly.
