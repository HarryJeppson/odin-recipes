Description:

Chocolate chip house cookies!

For 4 people

Ingredients:

  • 85g of butter
  • 85g of sugar
  • 1x bag of vanilla sugar
  • 150g of flower
  • 100g of dark chocolate
  • 1x tea spoon of salt
  • 1x tea spoon of baking powder
  • 1x egg

Steps:

  1. Chop the chocolate into small chips.
  2. Preheat the oven to 180°C (thermostat 6). In a mixing bowl, add 75 g of butter, the sugar, the whole egg, and the vanilla. Mix everything with a wooden spoon.
  3. Gradually add the flour mixed with the baking powder, the salt, and the chocolate chips.
  4. Using a paper towel, butter a baking tray and place the cookie dough on it.
  5. To form the cookies, use two tablespoons to make small heaps, spaced apart—they will spread as they bake.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes.
