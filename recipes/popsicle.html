Popsicle

Description:

Cold and frooty popsicles!

For 6 people

Ingredients:

  • Fruit syrop
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Prepare: an ice cube tray with sticks, or a special popsicle mold.
  2. Dilute some syrup with water as you normally would.
  3. Pour the mixture into your mold (with a stick).
  4. Place in the freezer for at least 3 hours.
  5. To remove from the mold:
  6. – Either gently pull the popsicle out of the mold to avoid breaking it.
  7. – Or, be more cautious: dip the bottom of the mold in warm water for a short moment, or let it sit at room temperature for 5 minutes before removing.
